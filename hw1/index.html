What is Web Development?

Have you ever wondered how websites are created? Every website you visit, whether it is YouTube, Amazon, or your school's website, is built through web development. Web development combines creativity and technology to create websites that people can use every day.

Web developers use different tools and programming languages to build websites. HTML provides the structure of a webpage, while CSS controls its appearance. Together, these technologies help create websites that are organized, attractive, and easy to navigate.

Why Is Web Development Important?

Today, people use websites for almost everything, from learning and shopping to entertainment and communication. Web development makes these websites possible by creating pages that are organized, functional, and easy to navigate. Without web development, many of the online services people use every day would not exist.

Web development is also important because it helps businesses, schools, and organizations connect with people around the world. As more people rely on the internet, the need for well-designed, user-friendly, and accessible websites continues to grow.

Popular Types of Websites

Social Media

Social media websites allow people to connect, communicate, and share content with others online. Users can post photos, videos, messages, and updates while interacting with friends, family, and communities around the world.

E-commerce

Online stores allow customers to browse and purchase products and services.

Educational

Educational websites provide learning materials and online courses.

News

News websites keep people informed by sharing current events, breaking news, and stories from around the world.
